My Favorite Food & Recipes from Different Cultures

Food is one of my favorite ways to experience different cultures. I especially enjoy Middle Eastern, Italian, and Asian cuisines.

1. Middle Eastern Cuisine

Middle Eastern cuisine is known for its rich flavors and diverse ingredients. Some of my favorite Middle Eastern dishes include:

  • Hummus
  • Kibbeh
  • Shawarma
  • Tabbouleh
  • Baklava

2. Italian Cuisine

Italian cuisine is known for its simplicity and use of fresh ingredients. Some of my favorite Italian dishes include:

  • Pizza
  • Pasta
  • Risotto
  • Lasagna
  • Tiramisu

3. Asian Cuisine

Asian cuisine is diverse and flavorful, with each country having its own unique dishes and cooking techniques. Some of my favorite Asian dishes include:

  • Sushi
  • Noodles
  • Tteokbokki
  • Spring Rolls
  • Matcha
